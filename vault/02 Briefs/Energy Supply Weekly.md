## Storage and inventories

- EU gas storage reached 62.3% full, a 3.1-point rise week-on-week, leaving the bloc ahead
  of the typical pre-summer refill pace ([[natural-gas-storage-eu]]).
- US commercial crude inventories fell by 1.2 million barrels to 421.4 million, a small draw
  rather than a sharp swing ([[crude-oil-inventories]]).

## Prices

- TTF gas settled at €34.2/MWh, down 2.0% on the week, consistent with the comfortable
  storage picture ([[energy-prices]]).
- German baseload power edged up 1.3% to €88.5/MWh, moving against gas rather than with it
  this week ([[energy-prices]]).
- At the pump, diesel averaged €1.71/L (+0.6%) and E10 €1.74/L (+0.4%), a quiet week for
  road-fuel costs ([[fuel-prices]]).
