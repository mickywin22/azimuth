> outbreak notifications** — the disease, the place, the case count and the alert level as the

## Reading the week

- One disease is the story of the slate: measles accounts for over half of all active
  notifications, including the only warning-level entry (Sudan, 300 cases). azimuth reports
  the notifications as the reporting bodies published them — disease, location, case count,
  alert level — and attaches no assessment of risk, spread or response; the record is the
  product ([[disease-outbreaks]]).
