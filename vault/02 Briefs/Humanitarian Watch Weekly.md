# Humanitarian Watch Weekly

> displacement statistics** — a refugee or IDP population count is an observed figure

## Honest scope — annual statistics, weekly heartbeat

- UNHCR publishes these statistics on an **annual reference year** (currently 2025) with
  periodic revisions — so week-on-week this brief typically holds flat and moves when the
  source revises or rolls the year. The weekly cycle records that heartbeat honestly: a flat
  week is the source being stable, not the brief being stale ([[displacement-flows]]).
- The 07-16 through 07-23 ingest cycles each confirmed the same aggregate totals
  (106,175,975 total / 64,239,352 IDPs / 28,461,306 refugees / 8,998,097 asylum seekers /
  4,477,220 stateless), consistent with the annual-snapshot cadence. The 2026-07-23 pull
  extended this run without revision ([[displacement-flows]]).

## Reading the week

- Two of every three forcibly displaced people in the 2025 published year are displaced
  *within* their own country — the 64,239,352 IDP figure dwarfs the cross-border refugee
  count of 28,461,306. azimuth reports the totals, the categories and the corridors as the
  UN agencies publish them, and attaches no forecast and no attribution of blame
  ([[displacement-flows]]).
- The 2026-07-23 pull surfaces the per-country IDP stocks in sharper detail. **Sudan**
  records the largest single IDP population in the set at **9,139,309** — ahead of Syria's
  5,542,227 and Myanmar's 3,628,840. **Ukraine** carries the largest refugee stock at
  **5,224,131**, followed by Syria at 4,865,764 and Sudan at 2,820,737. **Afghanistan**
  records no IDP figure in the published data but carries 2,671,560 refugees and 270,007
  asylum-seekers. These are recorded stocks as the source publishes them; no move since the
  prior published year is implied ([[displacement-flows]]).
- The global aggregate totals held flat across the full W30 window (07-16 through 07-23),
  consistent with the annual-snapshot cadence of UNHCR/OCHA publishing. The per-country
  detail rows above reflect the source as ingested on 2026-07-23 ([[displacement-flows]]).
