Four node kinds, drawn straight from the live bundle. A channel (hexagon) holds its L2 brief (large circle), which rests on the dated L1 source notes (small dots) its claims come from. Around them sits the entity layer scanned out of the real text: commodities (squares) and individual earthquake events (triangles) give each channel its texture, and shared regions (gold diamonds) bridge channels — a place the live data records under more than one theme, joined by gold edges.
